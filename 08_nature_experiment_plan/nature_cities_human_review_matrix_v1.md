# Nature Cities Human Review Matrix v1
Date: 2026-07-14

## Purpose

This matrix is the final human-review aid for the locked four-figure Nature Cities package. It is meant to be used after the sendable checklist, not before it.

## Figure-to-Manuscript Alignment

### Figure 1

- Manuscript sections:
  - Introduction: urban monitoring debt and observability as the central problem
  - Results: urban audit construction and region inventory
- Review question:
  - Does the figure clearly explain why observability failure is an urban problem rather than a technical note?

### Figure 3

### Figure 4

## Supporting Assets

- Fig. 5, Fig. 6, and Fig. 7 remain supporting assets only.
- They should appear in Extended Data or internal archive material, not as part of the four main Nature Cities figures.

## Release Consistency

- The release candidate, final bundle, submission index, and figure source map should all agree on the four-figure main set.
- The main manuscript should explicitly narrate Figures 1 through 4 and should not revert to a seven-figure main-story arc.

## Human Review Questions

1. Are the four main figures understandable without reading the methods first?
2. Does the abstract stay focused on people and built-up land rather than transport?
3. Does the conclusion read like an urban planning / monitoring-debt finding?
4. Are the supporting figures clearly demoted to Extended Data or archive use?

## If the Answer Is Yes

- The package is ready for final send.

## If the Answer Is No

- Fix only the mismatch that breaks the locked structure.
- Do not reopen the journal strategy or the lead-case framing.
