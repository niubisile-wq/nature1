## What Nature already wins

1. Multi-region observability-bias story.
2. Decision-facing exposure translation across people, built-up area, and transport.
3. Transfer-aware evidence stack.
4. Reviewer-defense structure: claim map, technical validation, risk audit, release-gap memo, and comparison scorecard.

## What still prevents a clean overall win

1. The manuscript still has `[DOI]` and `[REPOSITORY_URL]` placeholders.
2. The all-region hierarchical artifact is a closure-by-compilation, not a fresh new fit.
3. The release package is DOI-staged but not DOI-minted.

## Reviewer-facing conclusion

Nature is already the strongest scientific package in the set. It becomes the clean overall winner only when the release placeholders are replaced and the all-region hierarchy is described at the correct level of caution.
