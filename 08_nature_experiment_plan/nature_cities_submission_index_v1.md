## What Goes Into the Submission

### Presubmission enquiry

Use:

- proposed title
- 150-word summary
- why Nature Cities
- distinction from the existing Nature Cities subsidence paper
- novelty bullets

### Cover letter

Use:

- core positioning
- why Nature Cities
- what is new
- why this is not the existing US subsidence paper
- explicit boundary handling for transfer, transport and EGMS closure

### Main manuscript

Use:

- title
- abstract
- introduction
- results
- discussion
- methods
- data availability
- code availability

### Figures

Use:

- 4 main figures
- Extended Data tables for transport, transfer, robustness, and release provenance

## Locked Messaging

- The paper is about urban exposure accounting.
- Public monitoring gaps can undercount people and built-up land: 3.63 million hidden people and 422.78 km2 hidden built-up land in the Chao Phraya lead case.

- Transfer probes remain bounded and explicit.
- EGMS remains an external closure route, not a current claim.
